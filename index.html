About Me

I am a Theoretical Physicist born on 14 March 1879 in Ulm, German Empire.

I am praised for bring one of the greatest and most influential physicists of all time.

I am best known for developing the theory of relativity, but my other important contributions include the development of the theory of quantum mechanics.

Remember E=mc2, Yeah I formulated that!.

My Hobbies

My most favorite hobby is Thinking!

I also like doing puzzles, reading books about nature, and playing violin.

I would go sailing short distances and over quiet water.
